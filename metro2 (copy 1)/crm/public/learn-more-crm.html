Evolve.Ai CRM gives credit repair companies everything they need to manage clients, automate disputes, and scale their business. Multi-tenant architecture, AI-powered auditing, and a white-label client portal, all in one platform.

Platform Features

Everything a credit repair agency needs to operate efficiently and grow.

Client Management

Full CRM for managing up to unlimited clients with contact info, status tracking, document storage, and communication history.

AI-Powered Metro-2 Audits

Upload any credit report format. Our engine parses and audits every tradeline for Metro-2 compliance violations across all three bureaus.

Bulk Dispute Automation

Generate dispute letters for multiple clients at once. Batch processing saves hours of manual work for growing agencies.

Dispute Letter Engine

Legally compliant dispute letters auto-generated from audit results. Citations reference specific FCRA and Metro-2 standards.

White-Label Client Portal

Branded client portal with your logo, colors, and domain. Clients see their scores, disputes, documents, education, and messaging in one place.

Team Members

Add team members with role-based permissions. Manage who can access clients, generate letters, and view billing.

IntelliFeats Dispute Tracking

Advanced dispute outcome analytics. Track which disputes succeed, monitor timelines, and measure deletion rates across bureaus.

Tradeline Marketplace

Integrated marketplace for authorized user tradelines. Help clients build credit history faster with curated, high-quality primary tradelines.

Certified Mail

Send dispute letters via certified mail directly from the platform. Full tracking and delivery confirmation for every letter.

Data Breach Monitoring

HIBP-powered breach monitoring for your clients. Check exposure, alert clients, and use breach data to strengthen dispute arguments.

Affiliate Program

Built-in referral system with click tracking, commission calculations, and payout management. Your team and clients can earn by referring others.

Invoicing & Billing

Create and send invoices to clients. Stripe-integrated payment processing with billing plan management and payment tracking.
