How Specialists Are Ranked

Evolv doesn't let specialists buy their way to the top. Rankings are driven by real client outcomes, tracked inside the Evolv CRM.

📈

Dispute Success Rate

Percentage of disputed items removed or corrected across all clients. The single most important metric.

⚡

Avg. Response Time

How quickly the specialist responds to bureau replies and takes the next action. Speed matters in credit repair.

✅

Case Close Rate

The proportion of cases brought to a successful close. Measures follow-through, not just initial disputing.

⭐

Client Rating

Ratings left by real clients through the Evolv client portal — verified, not self-reported testimonials.

About Featured Placements

Some specialists may appear with a "Featured" badge. This indicates they have chosen to boost their visibility. However, boosted specialists can only appear in the top results if they maintain strong underlying performance scores — Evolv's algorithm caps how much a boost can affect placement.
