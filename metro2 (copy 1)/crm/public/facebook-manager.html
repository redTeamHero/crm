How to Set Up Facebook Integration

Follow these steps in order. Each step takes about 1–2 minutes. You'll need a Facebook account and a Facebook Business Page.

What you'll get at the end of this setup:
An App ID — a long number that identifies your Meta app
An App Secret — a private password for your Meta app (keep it confidential)
A connected Facebook Business Page so Evolv can publish posts on your behalf
1
Create a Meta Developer App
1a — App Details screen

Go to developers.facebook.com/apps and sign in. Click the blue Create App button. You'll land on "App Details" — give your app any name (e.g. Evolv Social), enter your email, then click Next.

1b — Use Cases screen

On the "Add use cases" screen, on the left side click Others. You'll see a list appear on the right. Select "Create an app without a use case" (the one that says "get an app ID without adding any permissions, features and products"). Click Next.

Note: Do not select "Other (This option is going away soon)" — use the one above it.

1c — Business & Remaining screens

If prompted to select a Business Account, pick yours from the dropdown (or skip if you don't have one set up). Click through the Requirements and Overview screens, then click Create App at the end. Facebook may ask you to confirm your password.

1d — Find your App ID & App Secret

Once the dashboard loads, your App ID is the number displayed at the very top of the page (under your app name). To find your App Secret, click Settings › Basic in the left sidebar — click the Show button next to App Secret to reveal it. Copy both — you'll need them in Step 5.

2
Add the Facebook Login Product

Still in your App Dashboard, scroll down to find the Add Products section. Find "Facebook Login for Business" and click Set Up. This is required before you can add the redirect URL in the next step.

3
Add the OAuth Redirect URL

In the left sidebar, go to Facebook Login for Business › Settings. Find the "Valid OAuth Redirect URIs" field and add this exact URL:

Click Save Changes after adding the URL.

4
Enable the Required Permissions

In the left sidebar, go to App Review › Permissions and Features. Search for and add these three permissions: pages_manage_postspages_read_engagementpages_show_list

For each one, click "Add" and then set it to "Advanced Access". You can test with just your own page first — full App Review is only needed if you want to manage pages owned by other people.

5
Enter Your Credentials in Evolv

Go to Settings → Social Media in Evolv's sidebar. Paste your App ID and App Secret from Step 1 into the fields there and click Save. Once saved, come back to this page — the Connect Facebook Page button will appear above.

6
Connect Your Facebook Business Page

Click the Connect Facebook Page button that now appears above. A Facebook authorization window will open — log in if prompted, grant the requested permissions, and then select which Business Page you want Evolv to publish to. Your page access token is saved securely and the Status badge above will turn green.
