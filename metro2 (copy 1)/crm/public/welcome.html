Authorized User Tradelines

Looking for a faster way to build credit history? Tradelines can help you piggyback on established credit accounts.

What Are Tradelines?

A tradeline is any credit account that appears on your credit report. When you become an "authorized user" on someone else's credit card, their positive payment history can be added to your report.

This strategy - sometimes called "piggybacking" - can quickly boost your credit score by adding years of positive payment history, low utilization, and higher credit limits to your profile.

Our curated tradelines come from accounts with perfect payment histories, low utilization, and long credit ages - the key factors credit bureaus use to calculate your score.
