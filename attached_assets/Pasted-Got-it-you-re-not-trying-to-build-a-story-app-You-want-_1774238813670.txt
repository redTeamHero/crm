1. Idle State (Looping Scene)

	•	A pulse appears on a random city
	•	Thin line path (like flight path)
	•	Camera smoothly zooms in
	•	Slight screen shake or bass hit (optional)

3. Arrival (Focus State)
	•	Camera stops above city
	•	Slight tilt for cinematic angle
	•	Background dims slightly
	•	UI activates

🧩 Side Panel System (THIS is your main UI)

You’ll have two panels max:

⸻

📍 LEFT PANEL → “CITY INFO”

Slides in from left when city is selected.

Content:
	•	City name + country
	•	Coordinates (adds that premium feel)
	•	Tagline (1 line only)
	•	Small stats (clean + minimal)

Example:

DALLAS, TX
32.7767° N, 96.7970° W

A hub of growth and opportunity.

• Population: 1.3M+
• Key Industry: Finance / Tech
• Growth Rate: High

👉 Keep it tight, not cluttered

⸻

📊 RIGHT PANEL → “TIMELINE / STORY”

Slides in from right.

Style options:

Option A: Vertical timeline
	•	Clean dots + years
	•	Minimal text

Option B: Card stack
	•	Each event = card
	•	Slight parallax on scroll

Option C (🔥 best for cinematic):

Progressive reveal timeline
	•	Auto-plays like a story
	•	Each step fades in

Example:

1960s → Expansion boom
1980s → Corporate growth
2000s → Tech integration
Today → Financial powerhouse
